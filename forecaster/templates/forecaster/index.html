Algo Process


VIX:    The VIX is known was the fear index. It measures 30-day voltality, and investor sentiment. In general, when the VIX goes up, a recession is more likely to occur.

Yield Curve:    The yield curve is a collection of treasury bond yields for varying periods of time. In general, when they start to go down, a recession is more likely.

Prices:    One of the biggest tell-tale signs of a recession is, of course, falling stock prices.
